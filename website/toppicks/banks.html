Banks - Waiting Game

If what we've heard from the Weeknd's forthcoming album Kiss Land is any indication, it seems Abel Tesfaye won't be abandoning his trademark libidinous, downcast R&B sound anytime soon, so it's no wonder that he's taking shadowy L.A. singer Banks out on tour with him this fall. Pricking up ears last spring with her deeply seductive, Totally Enormous Extinct Dinosaurs-produced track "Warm Water", Banks is now set release her debut EP London on September 10. "Waiting Game", the first of the EP's four tracks offered up, is a sullen and starkly pretty introduction to Banks' fragile, alluring vocals. "I'm thinking it over/ The way you make me feel all sexy, but it's causing me shame," she sings-- a sentiment that, of course, recalls her brooding tourmate. Vienna-by-way-of-London producer Sohn mixes things up by dropping in some blown-out, digitized throbs, which effectively reinforces the track's sunken loneliness.
